How to Play Nordle
Nordle is like Wordle, but with multiple boards! Guess the words on all boards simultaneously.

After each guess, each board will give you feedback. The color of the tiles will show how close you are for that board:

Rules:
  • Guess a word, and it applies to all boards.
  • Each board will provide feedback based on the hidden word for that board:
    • Green: Correct letter and position.
    • Yellow: Correct letter, wrong position.
    • Gray: Letter not in the word.
  • Win by solving all boards in the fewest guesses possible!
